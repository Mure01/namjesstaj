iDEALAN NAMJEŠTAJ
ZA VAŠ DOM

Uz kuhinje modernog dizajna i visoke kvalitete, opremamo apartmane i montiramo namještaj za poslovne prostore, dječje i spavaće sobe.

Također možemo Vam ponuditi ugradnju ugradbenih ormara, kliznih vrata i kliznih stijena, te montaže iskoristivih podnih konstrukcija koje su idealna rješenja za visoke i vrlo visoke stanove, prema rješenjima vašeg ili našeg dizajnera.

KOMPLETNO OPREMANJE

Opremanje enterijera zahtjeva stručnost, inspiraciju i slobodu kreativnosti. Trendovi budućnosti diktiraju spoj struke i individualno-kreativnih ideja.

Nesumnjivo prateći savremene trendove iDEAL nudi platformu zajedničke kreativnosti i stvaranja u vašem domu. Spojem dizajnerske stručnosti i vaših ličnih kreacija nastaju umjetnička djela u obliku modernog namještaja za vaš dom.

Pogodnosti kompletnog opremanja:

  • Svi modeli namještaja su po mjeri
  • Sručno savjetovanje o opremanju
  • 3D idejna rješenja svih vaših želja
  • Najkvalitetniji materijali za izradu namještaja
  • Autentičan dizajn i stil
  • Bogat izbor dekora
  • Izbor materijala za izradu pločastog namještaja (iveral, lakirani medijapan)
  • Brza isporuka

iDEALNA KUPOVINA

U službi povjerenja naših vjernih kupaca, koje nas čini jednim od lidera u proizvodnji i prodaji namještaja po mjeri, od sada potpuno nova iDEAL-na usluga online kupovine iz udobnosti vašeg doma.

Na našoj web stranici kupci imaju mogućnost kupovine i konfiguracije namještaja po želji iz iDEAL izuzetno bogate ponude.

Savjeti i pomoć

Treba li vam savjet ili pomoć u bilo kojem trenutku, prije, tokom ili nakon kupnje? Pomoć možete zatražiti osobnim dolaskom u prodajne salone, putem telefona ili e-maila.

Dostava

Svu kupljenu robu isporučujemo vlastitim prevozom i to besplatno do 50 km udaljenosti, a preko u dogovoru sa kupcem.

Montaža

Za montažu namještaja kod kojeg je preporučljivo unajmiti stručnjaka (kao što je slučaj kod spavaćih soba, Kuhinja itd.), rado ćemo Vam pomoći i ponuditi da Vam naši monteri namontiraju namještaj po povoljnim cijenama

Garancija

Nudimo granciju na namještaj 12 mjeseci, ako se budete ravnali prema datim uputama. Na bijelu tehniku (BEKO) garancija je 5 godina.

iDEAL FURNITURE
FOR YOUR HOME

In addition to kitchens of modern design and high quality, we equip apartments and assemble furniture for business premises, children's and bedrooms.

We can also offer you the installation of built-in wardrobes, sliding doors and sliding walls, as well as the installation of usable floor structures that are ideal solutions for tall and very tall apartments, according to your or our designer's solutions.

COMPLETE EQUIPMENT

Furnishing the interior requires expertise, inspiration and freedom of creativity. Future trends dictate the combination of expertise and individual creative ideas.

Undoubtedly following modern trends, iDEAL offers a platform of joint creativity and creation in your home. The combination of design expertise and your personal creations creates works of art in the form of modern furniture for your home.

Advantages of complete equipment:

  • All furniture models are custom-made
  • Professional advice on furnishing
  • 3D conceptual solutions of all your wishes
  • The highest quality materials for making furniture
  • Authentic design and style
  • A rich selection of decor
  • Selection of materials for the production of panel furniture (chipboard, lacquered MDF)
  • Fast delivery

iDEAL BUY

In the service of the trust of our loyal customers, which makes us one of the leaders in the production and sale of custom-made furniture, from now on the brand new iDEAL online shopping service from the comfort of your home.

On our website, customers have the option of purchasing and configuring furniture as desired from iDEAL's extremely rich offer.

Advice and help

Do you need advice or help at any time, before, during or after your purchase? You can ask for help by personally coming to the showrooms, by phone or e-mail.

Delivery

We deliver all purchased goods with our own transport, free of charge up to a distance of 50 km, and beyond in agreement with the customer.

Assembly

For the assembly of furniture where it is advisable to hire a specialist (such as in the case of bedrooms, kitchens, etc.), we will be happy to help you and offer to have our fitters install the furniture for you at favorable prices

Guarantee

We offer a warranty on furniture for 12 months, if you follow the instructions given. White goods (BEKO) are guaranteed for 5 years.

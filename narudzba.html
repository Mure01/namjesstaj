iDEAL NARUDŽBA

NAPOMENA:
Prilikom narudžbe u polje "naziv artikla" unosite ID koji se nalazi ispod artikla koji ste odabrali.
U polje "sirina" unosite širinu koju imate dostupnu za proizvod, ukoliko se radi o kuhinji, ormaru ili proizvodu koji može biti ograničen dimenzijama, u suprotnom u polje unesite 1.
U polje "zahtjevi.." unosite podatke koje biste eventualno promjenuli, npr boju namještaja, izbacili neke elementa, a ubacili druge itd.
Vaši podaci ostaju sigurni i neće biti zloupotrebljeni.

iDEAL ORDER

NOTE:
When ordering, in the "item name" field, enter the ID found under the item you selected.
In the "width" field, enter the width you have available for the product, if it is a kitchen, wardrobe or product that can be limited in dimensions, otherwise enter 1 in the field.
In the "requirements.." field, enter data that you would eventually change, for example the color of the furniture, remove some elements and insert others, etc.
Your data remains safe and will not be misused.
